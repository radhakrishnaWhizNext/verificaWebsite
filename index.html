About Us

VERIFICA the Credential authentication services organization is one of the pioneers in instant digital authentication services in the country. We are a highly reliable digital SAAS platform, providing ratings, risk assessment and analytical solutions to Individuals, Small & Medium Enterprises and Corporates. Our platform acts as one-stop-shop for Enterprises and hiring agencies to screen and assess prospective candidates on a well-defined set of records such as Academy, Employment, Criminal history, Social DNA, Government Identification and Personality profiling. Verifica is leveraging the best in class technology tools for today and tomorrow to meet the enterprise’s ever increasing needs and expectations.

Verifica’s on-the-tap digital instant authentication SAAS platform offers its services to all business verticals, enterprises and hiring agencies to quickly put a check to deceitful candidatures by offering enterprise level security and authentication. Verifica is grounds-up platform and can be customized to suit individual enterprise's needs, process and philosophy. All these features and functions are offered at zero upfront cost to enterprises. Interfaces

Verifica makes you relax to screen and hire at ease, as Verifica uses the following interfaces and multiple data point to get you Mr./Ms.Right.

Academy Records

Be it school, Degree, PG and Doctoral academic records, all at a touch of a button with the individual's consent to submit the records from the original source to the requestor.

Employment Records

Verifica does analytics on multiple data points to fetch the correct employment records by using AI, Analytic and Block chain tools from multiple sources and ledgers.

Criminal Records

Verifica looks at the country level data base to get the criminal records.



Social behavior

Verifica utilizes data science tools viz., AI and Advanced analytics to decipher the Social behaviour of individual to determine the suitability of the candidate for specific job and function.

Government Identification

Verifica uses identity management tools to determine the correct identity of individual (PAN, AADHAR, DL, Voter ID Etc.,)

Personality Discovering

Individuals can discover their personality based on small test at a nominal price. That helps individuals to determine the type of job that they are suitable.
